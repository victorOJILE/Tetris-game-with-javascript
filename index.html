How to play?

Try to arrange the tetrominoes to completely fill up a line or more (no empty slot in that line).

Controls 1:

  1. Move to the left with the left arrow.
  2. Move to the right with the right arrow.
  3. Change shape with the up arrow.
  4. Drop down with the down arrow.
  5. Press the spacebar key or ESC to pause game.

Controls 2:

  1. Click the top of the screen to change shape.
  2. Click the middle left of the screen to move to the left.
  3. Click the middle right of the screen to move to the right.
  4. Click the bottom of the screen to drop down.
